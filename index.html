About SAFE

Established in 1976 in Vadodara, SAFE Pvt. Ltd. has emerged as a leading manufacturer of top-quality Stainless Steel Wire Mesh, Extruder Screens, Spin Pack Filters, and Polymer Candle Filters. With over four decades of expertise, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative solutions that meet the diverse needs of our global clientele.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our state-of-the-art manufacturing processes, stringent quality checks, and continuous innovation. We are recognized as one of the Top 10 Major Enterprises in the Global Market of Insect Screen Analysis.
